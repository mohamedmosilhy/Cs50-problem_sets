The Darbuka Instrument

Darbuka instrument has a history that goes all the way back to the Common Era in Egypt and comes in different shapes
and sizes. It is also very common in Anatolia, Mesopotamia, Arabic countries and North Africa. It is widely played
in Turkish Folk Music amongst other instruments or solo and has also come to known as an important instrument in the
Turkish classical music lately. This joyful instrument has different names in different regions such as “dümbek,”
“dümbelek”, “tömbek” but its original name “dümbelek” is actually an Arabic name which originally means “to strike”.
